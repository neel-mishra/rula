# Metrics and Compounding

## Objective

Measure whether the engineering system is becoming easier, safer, and faster over time.

## KPI Groups

### Flow and Speed
- Plan-to-merge cycle time
- Review-to-merge cycle time
- Rework rate per merged change

### Quality and Reliability
- Defect escape rate
- P1/P2 findings per change
- Rollback frequency

### Compounding Strength
- Number of compound artifacts per week
- Template/checklist/rule updates per week
- Reuse rate of prior solution patterns

### Governance and Confidence
- High-risk gate pass rate
- Maturity score delta by domain
- Audit artifact completeness rate

## Weekly Ritual (Required)

1. Review KPIs and anomalies.
2. Review incidents and high-severity findings.
3. Update maturity scorecards.
4. Convert learnings into system updates.
5. Assign next-week improvement actions.

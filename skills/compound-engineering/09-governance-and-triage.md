# Governance and Triage

## Priority Model

- P1 (must fix): blocks merge/release
- P2 (should fix): fix before merge unless explicitly deferred
- P3 (nice to fix): optional, queue for compounding backlog

## Ownership Model

- Single feature owner is accountable for:
  - plan quality
  - review closure
  - risk decisions
  - compound artifact publication

## SLA Guidance

- P1: immediate; merge blocked
- P2: resolve before merge or assign due date
- P3: queue with owner and target review cycle

## Escalation Rules

- Unresolved P1: automatic stop.
- Repeated P2 in same domain: escalate to weekly compounding agenda.
- Same root cause recurring twice: mandatory prevention-rule update.

## Deferral Policy

A deferred item must include:
- explicit owner
- due date
- risk impact
- reason for deferral

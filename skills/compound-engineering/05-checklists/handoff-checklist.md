# Handoff Checklist

## Context
- [ ] Current status documented
- [ ] Completed vs remaining work listed
- [ ] Dependencies and blockers listed

## Operational Continuity
- [ ] How-to-continue steps are explicit
- [ ] Validation steps included
- [ ] Rollback and risk notes included

## Ownership
- [ ] New owner acknowledged
- [ ] SLA or due dates captured
- [ ] Related artifacts linked (plan/review/todos/compound)

## Synthesis summary
- Users want inbox triage, draft assistance, and digest-style briefing.
- High-value requirement: user keeps control of sending decisions.
- Main pain points: overload, context switching, missed follow-ups, trust concerns.
- Winning product attributes: explainable triage, safe automation, strong privacy posture.

## Implications for build
- Prototype should focus on Gmail + triage + drafts + brief only.
- MVP should add RAG personalization and stronger policy controls.
- Production should prioritize reliability, compliance, and kill-switch safety.

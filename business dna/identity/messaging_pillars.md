# Messaging Pillars: Rula

All external and GTM-system content should anchor to one or more pillars below. Pillars 1-3 emphasize patient/member outcomes; pillars 4-5 emphasize partner-scale value; pillar 6 enforces evidence discipline.

---

## Outcome Pillars

### Pillar 1: Access Without Delay
- **Core argument:** People should be able to find in-network mental healthcare quickly, including rapid appointment availability.
- **Proof style:** Access speed, provider breadth, and matching capability.
- **Hook examples:** "Get matched with care that fits your needs." / "Start care as soon as tomorrow."

### Pillar 2: Insurance Simplicity and Affordability Clarity
- **Core argument:** Coverage and expected costs should be transparent before treatment starts.
- **Proof style:** Coverage verification, average insured session-cost framing, accepted-insurance breadth.
- **Hook examples:** "Insurance made easy." / "Know your expected cost before your first session."

### Pillar 3: Progress-Oriented Clinical Care
- **Core argument:** High-quality care should drive meaningful symptom improvement over time.
- **Proof style:** Patient-reported improvement and PHQ/GAD progress claims where sourced.
- **Hook examples:** "Where access meets progress." / "Support that helps people feel better."

---

## Scale and Trust Pillars

### Pillar 4: Integrated Therapy + Psychiatry Journey
- **Core argument:** Patients benefit when therapy and medication pathways are coordinated and easy to manage.
- **Proof style:** Combined-care framing and platform continuity.
- **Use cases:** Care-navigation assets and treatment-path education.

### Pillar 5: Partner-Ready Access Infrastructure
- **Core argument:** Employers, plans, and systems need scalable access and measurable quality outcomes.
- **Proof style:** Partnership impact claims (utilization challenge, capacity extension, outcomes orientation).
- **Use cases:** B2B decks, partner pages, sales narratives, MAP planning context.

---

## Proof and GTM Alignment

### Pillar 6: Source-Backed Messaging Discipline
- **Core argument:** Claims must be sourced, current, and audience-appropriate.
- **Proof style:** URL-cited public metrics + case-study PDF logic for internal GTM operations.
- **Use cases:** Agent prompts, outbound copy, MAP evidence scoring notes, launch messaging.

---

### AI Agent Context Rule
Every output must map to at least one pillar:
- Patient-facing growth content: pillars 1, 2, or 3.
- Employer/partner GTM content: pillars 2, 5, and 6.
- Product and care-journey explainers: pillars 3 and 4.

When multiple pillars apply, lead with the one tied to the audience's immediate pain and decision point.

# Ad Copy Frameworks: Rula (Mental Healthcare Access)

Goal: Help GTM teams draft credible, segment-aware copy for patients, employers, and partners evaluating mental healthcare access and partnership solutions.

**Not in scope:** Unsourced clinical guarantees, stigmatizing language, or competitor-smear copy.

---

## 1. Demand Gen (Primary)

### Search (Google)
- **Intent:** Users and benefits stakeholders actively searching for therapy/psychiatry access, insurance coverage, and mental-health support options.
- **Headline patterns:** `[Outcome] + [insurance clarity]` or `[Need] -> [matched in-network care]`.
- **Description patterns:** Mention provider fit, cost transparency, and fast access when true.

### Paid Social (Meta, LinkedIn)
- **Strategy:** Problem-first hooks:
  - Patient: "can't find in-network care"
  - Employer: "benefits underutilization"
  - Partner: "capacity and outcomes pressure"
- **Visual linkage:** Align with `creative_direction.md`.
- **CTA:** Match audience maturity (get started, check coverage, talk to partnerships).

### Remarketing
- **Strategy:** Reinforce one proof per asset (match quality, insurance clarity, outcomes orientation).
- **Avoid:** Overloading assets with too many claims in one unit.

---

## 2. Lifecycle & Expansion

### Patient/member lifecycle email
- **Goal:** Move users from intent to first appointment and then continuity.
- **Tone:** Supportive, clear, non-clinical in tone but clinically responsible in wording.

### Employer/partner lifecycle
- **Goal:** Increase campaign execution quality and partner confidence.
- **Content:** Operational next steps, MAP milestones, and measurable adoption indicators.

---

## 3. Compliance & Honesty

- **Numbers:** Cite source URL or PDF for each metric used.
- **No guaranteed outcomes:** Use "can," "may," or sourced aggregate outcomes language.
- **Clinical language safety:** Do not imply diagnosis or treatment guarantees in ad copy.
- **Competitor posture:** Respectful, fit-based comparison only.

---

### AI Agent Context Rule
For every ad asset:
1. Pick 1-2 pillars from `messaging_pillars.md`.
2. Pick audience segment from `core/ideal_customer_profile.md`.
3. State proof source inline or in notes.
4. Keep claim-risk low: no unsourced hard promises.

If confidence in source evidence is low, route draft for human review.

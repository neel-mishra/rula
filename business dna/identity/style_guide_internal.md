# Internal Communication Style: Rula Team

## 1. The Core Philosophy
- **Standard:** Async-first, concise, source-backed.
- **Philosophy:** Respect attention and reduce ambiguity.
- **Execution:** Internal updates should make decisions faster, not create interpretation work.

---

## 2. Tone & Diction
- **Personality:** Clear and accountable collaborator.
- **Technicality:** Medium-high for GTM/RevOps/clinical ops topics; define acronyms at first mention.
- **Transparency:** Raise uncertainty and risks early, with suggested mitigation.

---

## 3. The "This, Not That" Internal Framework
| We Are... | But Never... | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data-backed** | Data-dumped | We need decisions, not raw-table overload. |
| **Actionable** | Abstract | Every update should include owner and next step. |
| **Cross-functional** | Siloed | Employer motion spans RevOps, Sales, Product, and Clinical contexts. |
| **Truthful about risk** | Optimistic hand-waving | MAP quality and forecast trust depend on honest signal reporting. |

---

## 4. Structured Messaging Templates

### Metric Alert (GTM/quality)
- **What changed:** State the metric and direction.
- **Why it matters:** One sentence linking to business impact.
- **Next step:** Owner + date + expected check-back.

### Customer Signal (AE/partner)
- **Account context:** Segment and status in funnel.
- **Signal:** Commitment strength, blocker, or change in likelihood.
- **Follow-up:** Action needed to advance MAP quality.

### Weekly GTM Update
- **Pipeline health:** Coverage and stage progression.
- **Output quality:** Agent acceptance/use and override signals.
- **Risk log:** Top 1-3 issues and mitigations.

---

## 5. Communication Rituals
- **Standups:** Async summaries with blockers first.
- **Weekly review:** Numbers first, narrative second.
- **Escalations:** Facts, impact, owner, deadline, unblock request.

---

### AI Agent Context Rule
Internal outputs must be brief, evidence-aware, and action-oriented:
- Include source link/reference for claims.
- Include explicit owner and due date for actions.
- If confidence is low, state uncertainty and escalation path.

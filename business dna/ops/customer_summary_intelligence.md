# Customer Summary Intelligence & Feedback Loop

## 1. Persona Deep-Dive
- **Primary persona:** Benefits decision-maker and employer-channel AE buyer committee.
- **Jobs to be done:** Increase covered mental-health utilization while preserving operational simplicity and partner trust.

## 2. Qualitative Insights (Voice of Customer)
- **Magic moments (capture examples):**
  - "We found in-network providers faster than expected."
  - "Cost expectations were clear before first sessions."
  - "Employees actually used the benefit once campaigns launched."
- **Friction points (capture examples):**
  - Ambiguous campaign commitments.
  - Delayed stakeholder buy-in after verbal intent.
  - Confusion between interest and firm MAP commitment.
- **Switch trigger:** Need for more reliable access/outcomes than existing point solutions or underperforming EAP pathways.

## 3. Feature/Request Backlog
- **High demand:** Better MAP evidence capture templates, cleaner campaign calendar visibility, confidence rationale in plain language.
- **Low demand/niche:** Highly customized reporting views without clear operator use case.

## 4. Quantitative Health
- **Output quality indicators:** AE acceptance/use rate for generated outreach, MAP false-positive rate, override rate.
- **Adoption indicators:** Campaign commitment depth (quarters), launch follow-through, repeat campaign execution.

---
### AI Agent Context Rule
When drafting outbound or summaries, pull exactly 3 signals from this file:
1. One magic moment.
2. One friction point.
3. One measurable indicator.

Match wording to actual stakeholder language from call notes whenever possible.
